Admin Panel for Venora Drinking Water

Problem Statement

To perform day to day operations VWD needed an automated system to maintan a record.VWD had to maintain a manual record in the past for their operations.

How This App Helps?

This app serves as a dashboard for admins @ VWD. Through this app admins can fulfil Orders, manage Stock, Products & Suppliers.Provides insights for Orders, Stock & Products to have a quick glance what's going on with system. Main purpose this app serves is that to automate the already existing manual system. Now customers can make orders from mobile app and these orders can be fulfilled using this dashboard.
